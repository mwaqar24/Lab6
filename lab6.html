Sweetheart gifts are thoughtful and sentimental presents given to express love and affection. They can range from personalized keepsakes, like engraved jewelry or custom photo frames, to romantic gestures such as handwritten letters, chocolates, or flowers. Whether for a special occasion like an anniversary or just to show appreciation, sweetheart gifts symbolize love, care, and deep emotional connection.
